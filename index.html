Intro

Stephen has over twenty years of experience in the film industry working on digital and film projects. He is available for roles such as Dailies Producer or Production Manager, Archive Restoration (16mm & 35mm), or as a freelance consultant.
Stephen is experienced in leading projects from camera acquisition (of any medium) through grading, editorial and delivery.

Based in London Stephen is available for work either in the UK or internationally.

About


Stephen started working for Todd AO as a runner in 1997. He spent 11 years in their film laboratories gaining indepth knowledge of every step of the film process. When Deluxe took over Todd AO he became their Film Technical Expert and Dailies Contact, working on a range of projects from commercials to features. Working for sister company Company 3 he then moved into producing, overseeing the Dailies process. During this period he also worked on repairing and restoring 16mm and 35mm film for various clients. Head hunted by Technicolor in 2015 he then moved into a Senior Producer role, followed by Dailies Production Manager.
Stephen then went on to work on everything from editorial to broadcast for longform and shortform projects.
